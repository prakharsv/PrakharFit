Credentials & Certifications

Before you trust someone with your health, you deserve to know exactly who you are working with.

When I decided to become a coach, I did not settle for the minimum. The personal transformation was not enough I needed to understand the science behind what had happened to my own body, and I needed credentials that held me to the highest standard of knowledge and safety. Every qualification below was earned for that reason.

Personal Training Credentials

These are the foundational qualifications that certify me to design and deliver safe, effective training programs.

PT & Nutrition Diploma

Diploma in Personal Training & Nutrition the comprehensive foundation covering anatomy, programming, and dietary science.

Level 4 CPT

Certified Personal Trainer Certificate at Level 4 the highest standard personal training qualification in the UK framework.

ACE CPT

ACE Certified Personal Trainer one of the most globally recognised and respected personal training certifications in the world.

Professional Memberships

Recognised bodies that hold me to ongoing standards of practice and continued professional development.

EREPS Level 4 Member

European Register of Exercise Professionals Level 4 membership, the European standard for qualified fitness professionals.

SPEFL-SC Level 4

Sports & Physical Education Federation of India Level 4 certified, recognising professional standards within India's fitness sector.

Nutrition Specialisations

What separates my approach is that training and nutrition are designed together. These qualifications are the reason I can do that.

Sports Nutrition Certificate

Specialist certification covering macronutrient strategy, sports fuelling, and nutrition periodisation for athletic performance.

Fitness Nutrition Specialist

ACE-approved Fitness Nutrition Specialist (2.1 CEC) covering evidence-based dietary approaches for fat loss, muscle gain, and general health.

Specialised Training Methods

Additional certifications that allow me to build varied, effective programs beyond standard resistance training.

Olympic Lifting

Certified in Olympic lifting technique and programming for clients who want to develop explosive strength and athletic movement.

Plyometrics

Certified in plyometric training power development, agility, and athletic conditioning for functional performance.

Kettlebell Training

Certified in kettlebell programming one of the most effective tools for combining strength, conditioning, and mobility in a single session.

Suspension Training

Certified in suspension training methods bodyweight-based training using instability to develop total-body strength and coordination.

Safety Certification

Non-negotiable in any coaching environment.

CPR Certified

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation certified because client safety in any training environment is the foundation everything else is built on.
