Common Questions

Straightforward answers about how online coaching works.

Is this fully online? Do I need to be in Bhopal?

Yes, both programs are 100% online. Your workout plan, nutrition guide, and all communication happen through WhatsApp and email. You can be anywhere in India and it will work exactly the same.

I have never lifted weights before. Is this too advanced for me?

I started at 120 kg with no gym experience. Every program I write begins from your actual level not an assumed one. You will learn correct form, build confidence progressively, and never be put in a position to get hurt or feel lost.

Can my meal plan include Indian food?

Absolutely and this is one of the things I do specifically well. As a Sports Nutrition Specialist from Bhopal, I build meal plans around Indian foods across vegetarian, vegan, and non-vegetarian diets. You will not be eating bland chicken and broccoli.

I have a very busy schedule. Can I realistically do this?

Yes. I practiced as an advocate before becoming a coach I know what a genuinely demanding schedule looks like. Programs are designed to fit the time you actually have. Most clients on the Complete Transformation train 4 to 5 times a week in 45 to 60-minute sessions.

What is the difference between the two plans, really?

The Essentials Plan gives you an expert-built structure to follow on your own. The Complete Transformation means I am actively managing and adjusting your program every week including your nutrition, your form, and your progress. The difference is the level of ongoing involvement and accountability.
