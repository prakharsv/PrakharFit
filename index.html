The Story Behind the Coach

I am not someone who was always fit. I know exactly what it feels like to start from zero.

Chapter 1: 120 kg and No Way Out

At my heaviest, I weighed 120 kg. College had been four years of a sedentary lifestyle, habitual binge eating, and zero accountability. The weight showed up everywhere: in how I carried myself, how I spoke in a room, how I made decisions. I was not just physically heavy. I was stuck. My confidence was at rock bottom and I had no idea where the floor was.

Chapter 2: Six Months That Changed Everything

I stopped waiting to feel ready and walked into a gym. I put myself under a trainer, committed fully for the first time in my life, and learned how nutrition and training actually work together. In six months, I lost 25 to 30 kg. Not through a crash diet or an extreme program, but through a structured, science-backed approach I could sustain. The scale changed. But so did everything else: my focus, my confidence, and my sense of what I was actually capable of. That transformation was not just about the weight. It was about understanding that the body responds predictably when you give it the right inputs.

Chapter 3: From Advocate to Coach

I spent years as a practicing advocate. The demands of legal work slowly pulled me back away from my health, a pattern I recognised in almost everyone around me. Smart, driven people with no time, no clear direction, and no one who understood both the science and the pressure of a real schedule. I enrolled in a certification programme and earned my ACE Certified Personal Trainer and Sports Nutrition Specialist credentials in six months, echoing the same timeline that first changed my life. The system I built for myself is now the system I build for others. Whether you want to lose fat, build muscle, or simply feel better, the principles are the same. What changes is how they are applied to you.

How the System Works

Four steps. Applied to your goal, your body, and your life.

1. Understand Your Starting Point

A detailed intake covering your health history, current habits, body composition, schedule, and goals. No assumptions. No templates. Only your data.

2. Build Your Nutrition Plan

A custom macronutrient strategy built around what you actually eat, including Indian meals across vegetarian, vegan, and non-vegetarian diets. Calibrated for your specific goal.

3. Train with Purpose

Evidence-based programming using progressive overload and functional movement. Calibrated to your current level, your goal, and the time you genuinely have available.

4. Become the Better Version

Strength. Energy. Confidence. Whatever brought you here, the outcome is a body and a routine that performs the way you want it to.
